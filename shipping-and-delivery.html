Shipping and Delivery

Awaredia Innovations is committed to delivering the highest quality service to our donors and supporters. While we primarily deal with donations and do not have physical products for sale, there may be instances where we provide physical items such as thank-you gifts, merchandise, or event tickets.

Shipping and Handling

For any physical items that require shipping, we will provide information on the shipping method, estimated delivery time, and any associated costs at the time of the transaction. We aim to dispatch items within 3-5 business days of the order being placed.

Delivery Times

Delivery times may vary depending on the destination and the shipping method chosen. We will provide an estimated delivery time when you place your order, but please note that this is an estimate and not a guarantee.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs, if applicable, will be calculated based on the size, weight, and destination of the order. We will provide a clear breakdown of any shipping costs before you finalize your transaction.

International Shipping

If we offer international shipping, additional charges may apply, and recipients are responsible for any customs or import duties that may be levied upon arrival in the destination country.

Tracking

Where possible, we will provide tracking information for your order so you can monitor its progress.

Issues and Concerns
